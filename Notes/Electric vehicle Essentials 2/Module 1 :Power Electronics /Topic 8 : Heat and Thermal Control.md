# Heat and Thermal Control
## Key point in TMS
- TMS ensures that the battery operated with a safe and optimal temperature range
- Various cooling methods can be employed , including air cooling , liquid cooling  and phase change materials (PCMs)
- Advanced TMS Can be integrated with vehicle HVAC ( Heating, Ventilaation and Air conditioning system)
- A well desgin TMS can improve the oveall efficiency of the EV
- Mordern TMS are often controlller by sophisticated software
- The TMS plays a crucial role in managing this heat, ensuring safe and efficient charging
- The materials used in TMS , such as coolants, need to be non-conductive and chemically stable
